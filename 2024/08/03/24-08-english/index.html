24/Aug 每日英语

1st

▶
The true delight is in the finding out rather than in the knowing.

真正的快乐在于发现，而非知道。

2nd

▶
Our deeds determine us, much as we determine our deeds.

什么样的人决定了做什么样的事，同样，做什么事决定了是什么样的人。

3rd

▶
The greatest discovery of any generation is that a human being can alter his life by altering his attitude.

任何时代最伟大的发现就是，人可以通过改变态度来改变自己的生活。

4th

▶
The tragedy of life is not so much what men suffer, but what they miss.

生活的悲剧不在于人们收到多少苦，而在于人们错过了什么。

5th

▶
A wise man is impartial, not neutral. A fool is neutral but not impartial.

君子周而不比，小人比而不周。

6th

▶
Success is a state of mind. If you want success, start thinking of yourself as a success.

成功是一种心境。如果你想获得成功，就先把自己看做的是成功的人。

7th

▶
The greatest barrier to success is the fear of failure.

成功最大的阻碍就是害怕失败。

8th

▶
Optimism is the faith that leads to achievement. Nothing can be done without hope and confidence.

乐观是通往成功的信念。没有希望和信息将一事无成。

9th

▶
Victory belongs to the most persevering.

胜利属于坚持到最后的人。

10th

▶
The man who has made up his mind to win will never say impossible.

凡是决心取得胜利的人，是从来不会说不可能的。

11th

▶
Nothing great was ever achieved without enthusiasm.

没有激情，任何伟大的事业不可能成功。

12th

▶
All that you do, do with your might; things done by halves, are never right.

凡事需尽力而为，半途而废者将永无成就。

13th

▶
Opportunity, sooner or later, comes to all who work and wish.

机会迟早会光顾那些努力工作又有志向的人。

14th

▶
The secret of success in life is for a man to be ready for his opportunity when it comes.

人生成功的秘诀就是当机遇来临时需要及时的抓住它。

15th

▶
A single sunbeam is enough to drive away many shadows.

一束阳光就足以驱散许多阴影。

16th

▶
Reading is equivalent to thinking with someone else's head instead of with one's own.

读书等于是借用他人的头脑来思考，而不是用自己的。

17th

▶
Man can climb to the highest summit, but he cannot dwell there long.

人可以爬到最高峰，但却不能在那儿久住。

18th

▶
The value of life lies not in the length of days, but in the use we make of them.

生命的价值不在于活了多少天，而在于我们如何使用这些日子。

19th

▶
Whenever you are from nature, keep to it; never desert your own line or talent.

无论你秉性如何，保持你的本色；永远不要抛弃你的天赋。

20th

▶
Weep no more, no sigh, nor groan. Sorrow calls no time that's gone.

别哭泣，别叹息，别呻吟；悲伤唤不回流逝的时光。

21th

▶
The great end of life is not knowledge but action.

人生的伟大目的不在于知而在于行。

22th

▶
Happiness is the spritual experience of living every minute with love, grace and gratitude.

幸福就是时时刻刻都以仁爱，善良和感恩来生活的精神体验。

23th

▶
There is, however, a limit at which forbearance cease to be a virtue.

忍耐也有个限度，超过了限度就不是美德。

24th

▶
Self-conquest is the greatest of all victories.

征服自己是一切胜利中的最伟大者。

25th

▶
Pride increases our enemies, but puts our fiends to flight.

骄傲找来敌人，驱走朋友。

26th

▶
Life is to talk after a respite and not to act ahead of schedule.

生命就是且缓一口气再讲，明天再说明天的。

27th

▶
Dream as if you will live forever. Live as if you will die today.

如永生般逐梦，如末日般生活。

28th

▶
Romance is the glamour which turns the dust of everyday life into a golden haze.

浪漫能让生活的尘埃变成金色的雾霭。

29th

▶
When the sun is shining I can do anything; no mountain is too high, no trouble too difficult to overcome.

当阳光明媚时，我可以做任何事情；没有一座山无法逾越，没有一件事件难以克服。

30th

▶
To avoid criticism, do nothing, say nothing and be nothing.

面对批判，什么都不要做，什么也不说，做个无足轻重的人。

31th

▶
What is man's first duty? The answer is brief; to be himself.

人的第一天职是什么；答案很简单，不伪装自己。
