24/Feb 每日英语

February

1st

▶
Life is a tragedy when seen in close-up, but a comedy in long-short.

人生近看是悲剧，远看是喜剧。

2nd

▶
Freedom lies in being bold.

自由源于勇敢。

3rd

▶
The only true wisdom is in knowing you know nothing.

唯一真正的智慧是知道你自己一所无知。

4th

▶
Mistakes are the portals of discovery.

错误是发现的大门。

5th

▶
A successful person is one who can lay a firm foundation with the bricks that others throw at him or her.

一个成功的人懂得将别人投掷过来的石头当做砖块，来建造牢固的地基。

6th

▶
Some of us think holding on makes us strong, but sometimes it is letting go.

我们中的一些人认为坚持会让我们变得强大，但有时，放手才是。

7th

▶
Don't watch the clock; do what it does. Keep going.

不要盯着时钟看，要向它一样行动，继续前行。

8th

▶
I learned that good judgment comes from experience and that experience grows out of mistakes.

我学到好的判断源自于经验，而经验源于错误。

9th

▶
Cease to struggle and you cease to live.

生命不止，奋斗不息。

10th

▶
Art is either plagiarism or revolution.

艺术要么是剽窃，要么是革命。

11th

▶
In solitude, be multitude to yourself.

在孤独中，一个人要像一支队伍。

12th

▶
Everyone is the master of his or her own fortune.

每个人都是自己命运的主宰。

13th

▶
A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush.

一鸟在手胜过双鸟在林。

14th

▶
Never be unduly elated by victory or depressed by defeat.

胜不骄，败不馁。

15th

▶
Almost anything is easier to get into than to get out of.

几乎所有的事情都是进去容易退出难。

16th

▶
We can give advice, but can't give conduct.

我们可以给忠告，但不能给指挥。

17th

▶
The great end of life is not knowledge but action.

人生的的伟大目的不在于知而在于行。

18th

▶
It's never too late to give up our prejudices.

抛弃我们的偏见。永远来得及。

19th

▶
A man can be destroyed but not defeated.

人可以被摧毁但不能被打败。

20th

▶
If you write one story, it may be bad; if you write a hundred, you have the odds in your favor.

如果你写一个故事，它可能很糟糕；但如果你写一百个，你便拥有更有利的胜算。

21th

▶
Courage is resistance to fear, mastery of fear - not absence of fear.

勇敢并非没有恐惧，而是克服恐惧，战胜恐惧。

22th

▶
Fear is the source of supersitition, and one of the main sources of cruelty. To conquer fear is the beginning of wisdom.

恐惧是迷信的根源，也是造成残忍的主要原因之一。智慧始于征服恐惧。

23th

▶
Living without an aim is like sailing without a compass.

生活无目标，犹如航海无指南针。

24th

▶
The only limit to our realization of tomorrow will be our doubts of today.

实现明天理想的唯一障碍是今天的疑虑。

25th

▶
Talking too much about oneself can also be a means to conceal oneself.

过多地谈论自己也可能是隐藏自己的一种手段。

26th

▶
Man cannot discover new oceans unless he has courage to lose sight of the shore.

人只有鼓起勇气告别海岸，才有可能发现新的海洋。

27th

▶
It never rains but it pours.

不鸣则已，一鸣惊人。

28th

▶
To strive, to seek, to find, and not to yield.

去奋斗，去追求，去发现，但不要放弃。

29th

▶
If you wait, all that happens is that you get older.

如果你等待，发生的只是你变老。
